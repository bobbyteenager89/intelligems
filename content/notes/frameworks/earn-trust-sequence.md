# Earn Trust Sequence

The operating sequence for a new consulting engagement:

1. **Information Receiving** — Listen first. Meet everyone. Ask questions. Don't prescribe.
2. **Information Giving** — Share insights from what you've learned. Connect patterns across conversations.
3. **Building Tangible Solutions** — Ship something. Guardian Bot, this workspace, actual deliverables.

## Why This Works

Coming in with prescriptions before earning trust = political liability. Coming in with questions and then solutions = credibility. The sequence builds trust through demonstrated listening and tangible output.
