# Three Revenue Levers

The three core ways to grow recurring revenue for a B2B SaaS:

1. **Lower customer acquisition costs** — improve targeting, messaging, and channel efficiency
2. **Decrease churn** — especially day 1 (onboarding failure) and day 7 (activation failure)
3. **Enable price optimization** — help customers find optimal price points to increase their revenue (and Intelligems' NRR)

## Why This Framework Matters

Most B2B SaaS companies focus 80% of effort on #1 while churn silently erodes the base. At Intelligems, all three are interconnected — better tests → better results → lower churn → higher LTV → can afford higher CAC.

## Application to Intelligems

- **Lower CAC:** Better positioning, agency partnerships, content that converts
- **Decrease churn:** Sequential onboarding, Guardian Bot (prevent broken test frustration), 90-day roadmaps
- **Price optimization:** Core product differentiation — "the best way to test pricing"
