# GEM Framework

**Growth Experiment Mindset** — Intelligems' internal framework for how customers should approach optimization.

## Three Pillars

1. **Experiment** — Run tests continuously. Never stop testing. Build hypotheses.
2. **Extend** — Apply learnings across channels and product lines. Don't silo insights.
3. **Explore** — Push into new areas. Shipping threshold → price → checkout → upsell stack.

## ICP Alignment

Intelligems' ideal customer is an "operator/founder who executes strategy" — someone who:
- Has context on their business (not a pure-play marketer)
- Makes decisions based on data
- Wants to understand *why*, not just *what*

Andrew fits this profile — which is the credibility angle for content and partnerships.

## Content Implications

Content that resonates: specific, tactical, opinionated. "We tested 47 shipping thresholds" not "The complete guide to shipping optimization."
