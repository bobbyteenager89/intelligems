# Competitive Landscape

## Primary Threat: Shoplift

- Shopify Plus certified
- AI-powered features as a differentiator
- Growing fast in the ecosystem

**Intelligems response:** Most robust Shopify-native platform with price testing. Core disadvantage is reactivation — hardest to pick back up after time away (product marketing challenge, not just product issue).

## Secondary Competitors

| Tool | Category | Threat Level | Notes |
|------|----------|--------------|-------|
| Rebuy | Upsells/Personalization | Medium | Expensive, black box AI, claims revenue credit unfairly |
| AfterSell | Post-purchase upsells | Low | Narrower scope |
| SellEasy | Upsells | Low | Good UX, no testing capability |
| Convert.com | A/B testing (general) | Medium | Enterprise-focused, less Shopify-native |
| VWO | A/B testing (general) | Low | Same — not Shopify-first |
| Triple Whale | Analytics | Low | Different category but competes for analytics budget |

## Intelligems Moat

- **Price testing** — genuinely differentiated, nobody else does it as well on Shopify
- **Statistical rigor** — proper experiment design, not just split URLs
- **Shopify-native** — built for how Shopify actually works
